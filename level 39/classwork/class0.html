Byzantine Empire

History Culture Legacy

History of Byzantium

The Byzantine Empire, also known as the Eastern Roman Empire, lasted for over a thousand years. It was a continuation of the Roman Empire in its eastern provinces, with its capital at Constantinople.

Culture and Religion

The Orthodox Christianity played a central role in Byzantine culture. Iconography, mosaics, and liturgical traditions reflected their devotion to the faith. Art and architecture flourished during this period, leaving a lasting legacy.

Legacy of Byzantium

The Byzantine Empire influenced medieval Europe, the Islamic world, and the Orthodox Church. Its contributions to law, art, and scholarship continue to inspire modern civilizations.
